About GreenLeaf Solar

Founded in 2015, GreenLeaf Solar has been at the forefront of Ethiopia's renewable energy revolution. Our mission is to make clean, affordable solar energy accessible to every home and business.

With a team of certified solar engineers and energy experts, we've completed over 1,200 installations across the country, helping our clients reduce their energy costs while protecting the environment.

We pride ourselves on using only the highest quality components from trusted manufacturers, backed by industry-leading warranties and our commitment to exceptional customer service.
