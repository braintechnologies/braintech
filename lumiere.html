Our Philosophy

At LUMINÈRE, we believe light is more than illumination—it's an emotional experience. Each piece is meticulously crafted to transform spaces into living art.

Founded in Milan by master designer Alessandro Moretti, our atelier combines centuries-old glassblowing techniques with cutting-edge technology.

Every creation undergoes 27 precise steps, from molten glass to final polish, ensuring unparalleled quality and luminosity.
